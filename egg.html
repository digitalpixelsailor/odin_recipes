Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs *2-4*
  • Olive oil
  • Lots of love

Steps

  1. Pour nice amount of olive oil to cover bottom of pan
  2. Keep stove on 3 - 4 heat through entire cook
  3. Wait a few minutes for oil to heat
  4. Crack 2 - 4 eggs into a bowl
  5. Mix yolks and rest of egg together
  6. Pour eggs into pan
  7. Occasionally move eggs with spatula
  8. Break eggs into pieces when cooked further
  9. Wait for eggs to completely cook to liking
  10. Turn off stove and move eggs to plate, bowl
  11. Add toppings *cut ham or pepper*
  12. Enjoy your scrambled eggs!
