Coffee

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans
  • Coffee blender *optional*
  • Milk/Creamer/Sweetener
  • Coffee brew machine
  • Sweetener *sugar, honey*

Steps

  1. Get bag of coffee beans
  2. Place beans in grinder *optional*
  3. Place beans in clean tamper for machine
  4. Add enough water for desired amount in the assigned area
  5. Put setting on to brew coffee
  6. Wait for coffee to brew
  7. Add coffee to cup, ration it for whatever you use
  8. Add milk or creamer, then add sugar or honey
  9. Mix it together with spoon, and enjoy!
